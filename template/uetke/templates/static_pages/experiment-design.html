Agile Software Development

At Uetke we are committed to deliver solutions through an Agile process. We believe that the best way to success is by following the four values of the manifesto:

  • Individuals and Interactions over processes and tools
  • Working Software over comprehensive documentation
  • Customer Collaboration over contract negotiation
  • Responding to Change over following a plan

We work side by side with senior and junior members of the lab. Through face-to-face discussions we will develop a full strategy regarding your experimental needs. We provide a comprehensive analysis, basing our conclusions not only on costs, but also on the availability of human resources, long term goals of the lab, and our own past experiences.

We can help you all the way from the writing of a proposal to the setup of the elements on an optical table, including the evaluation of different purchasing options. You should contact us if you doubt which microscope will answer your questions, or you need help calculating frame rates, resolution, etc.

Designing and building an experiment is a multi-stage task. If you are writing a proposal, you will get all the information you need for a complete experimental section, including calculations of resolution, frame rates, laser power, etc. If you are in the phase of building a setup, but lacking experienced researchers, we can build the microscope for you and train its users. We can also assist you if you need highly trained researchers for short periods of time to perform specific measurements.

Scientific work starts by choosing a question and follows by designing the experiments that will allow researchers to answer it. When designing an experiment, many different factors need to be taken into account. At Uetke we have built extensive expertise in problems related to microscopy.

We can assist you from the moment of writing a proposal to the setup of a microscope. Together with researchers and technicians, we can determine what are the best tools in order to achieve the best results. We can help you develop a timeline that fits with your plan and a budget for materials and services.
